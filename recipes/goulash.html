Goulash

Description

Goulash is a stew or soup made with meat, vegetables, and paprika.

It originated in Hungary and is a common dish in Central Europe.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Beef
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • Seasonings
  • Macaroni

Steps

  1. Cook the onion in oil, then add the ground beef and cook until brown. Add garlic.
  2. Stir in the water, tomatoes, and seasonings. Bring to a simmer.
  3. Stir in uncooked noodles, cover, and simmer until pasta is tender.
